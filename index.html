Tic-Tac-Battle!

Description:

The objective is to pick your spots and win over the boxes but if your plan fails the other player may win over your box and also your plan. BE Aware!

    Rules

  1. One Match, Three rounds(IF NECESSARY).
  2. Select three boxes total.
  3. Players pick their selections for each box.
  4. First player to match Three wins in Three consecutive boxes will win.
  5. Rounds will continue until a player wins with 3 boxes in a row or until all boxes are filled and a winner cannot be decided.
